About Us

STEM education curriculum, is based on the idea of educating students in four specific disciplines — Science, Technology, Engineering, Maths as an applied and interdisciplinary approach

Science - It is the study of the natural world.
Technology - It is related to any product made by humans to meet a want or need.
Engineering - It’s the design process used to solve problems.
Mathematics - It is the language of numbers, shapes, and quantities which seems to be irrelevant to many students.
STEM teaching is just not about grouping of subject areas but it helps to develop the deep mathematical and scientific ability, students required to compete in the next century workforce.
STEM methods along with preparing students for specific jobs It also develops uniqueness in thinking, reasoning, teamwork, investigative, and creative skills that students can use in all areas of their lives.
STEM Learning Academy’s objective is to intentionally incorporate different subjects across an existing curriculum to make teaching less time consuming, easy, and exciting.
